The LARGEST TENNIS TOURNAMENT



Wimbledon Championships 2023 is a Grand Slam tennis tournament that is taking place at the All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club in Wimbledon, London, United Kingdom. Wimbledon is the only major still played on grass, the traditional tennis playing surface. Also, it is the only Grand Slam that retains a night-time curfew
